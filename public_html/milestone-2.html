Explanation of WireFrames

Below is the detailed explanation of My Desktop Wireframe...

This Will Be The First Section (Mobile Wireframe 1)

At the top left of the screen, it will show my full name in a fancy font and on the top right it will have my nav bar with links to each section of the webpage, home, about, 5Step Healing and contact

Under my name and to the right of the page there will be a nice pic of me, and to the left I will have some information about myself and the benefit to the web visitor

Under my picture there will be one of my quotes (a word picture)

If you scroll down the page you will see a button that says, learn more about the 5Step Healing Process and there is a small description with an accompanying photo.

There is a second button in this same section that says, learn more about Trauma Drama Thursdays and there will be a small description with an accompanying photo.

This Will Be The Second Section (Mobile Wireframe 2)

This next section introduces the website visitor to my community, inviting them to a monthly zoom get together where they can find support, join the conversation and connect to other members of the community.

To the right of the community information, the site user can watch a clip of client testimonials, these testimonials come from community members

There is also a button that says, Zoom Link. If the site user wants to join the next zoom session they can click this link to get access to the zoom link

If you keep scrolling down you will come to the section that says, My Work... here there are four different video clips that showcase some of my speaking engagements and podcast appearances. Under each video is a short description of what it is. If the web user clicks on a video it give them the option to watch the full video on YouTube

This Will Be The Third Section (Mobile Wireframe 3)

This section highlights my 5Step Healing Process, there is a large photo that has the 5Steps on it (on the left) there is a brief description of what the 5Steps is all about and below is the large button that says, Get Access to the 5Steps

The section below this has a picture of me, with one of my most favorite quotes on self love.

Below, in my footer, will be links to all my social media sites, Instagram, X, LinkedIn, YouTube, Facebook and Clubhouse

The above description is the same for my Mobile Wireframes, except obviously, the info will be stacked instead of side by side, please see the Mobile Wireframe examples below.
